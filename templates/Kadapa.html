WELCOME TO KADAPA DISTRICT

Kadapa, known for its rich history and cultural heritage, offers a variety of historical monuments, temples, and natural attractions. It's a perfect destination for both history enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Places to visit in Kadapa

Ondo Caves

The Ondo Caves are a group of ancient rock-cut temples located near Kadapa. These caves are known for their fascinating architecture and historical significance.

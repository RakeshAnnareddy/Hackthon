WELCOME TO SRIKAKULAM DISTRICT

Srikakulam district is a district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, located in the Uttarandhra region of the state, with its headquarters located at Srikakulam.It is one of the six districts, located in the extreme northeastern direction of the state.

Places to visit in Srikakulam are

Sri Sri Sri Suryanarayana Swamy Temple

Located in Arasavalli, this ancient sun temple is one of the rare temples dedicated to Lord Surya. It is believed that the rays of the sun directly touch the feet of the deity twice a year, making it a unique spiritual experience.

Telukunchi Bird Sanctuary

Telukunchi Bird Sanctuary, located in the Ichchapuram mandal of Srikakulam district in Andhra Pradesh, India, is a small but important bird area known for its migratory birds, particularly open-billed storks, with villagers playing a key role in their protection.

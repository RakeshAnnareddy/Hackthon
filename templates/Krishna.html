Tourist Places in Krishna District

Amaravathi

Amaravathi is an ancient Buddhist site with ruins dating back to the 2nd century BCE. It's home to the famous Amaravathi Stupa.

Vijayawada

Vijayawada, located on the banks of the Krishna River, is known for its temples, historical monuments, and scenic beauty, such as the Prakasam Barrage.

View on Map

Kanaka Durga Temple

Located on Indrakeeladri Hill in Vijayawada, the Kanaka Durga Temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga and is one of the most visited temples in Andhra Pradesh.

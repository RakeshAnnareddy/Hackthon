Tourist Places in Guntur

Amaravati Temple

The Amaravati Temple is a significant pilgrimage site with a rich history. It is known for the famous Amaravati Stupa and ancient Buddhist ruins.

Puligundla Waterfalls

Nagarjuna Sagar

Nagarjuna Sagar is one of the largest and oldest dams in India, surrounded by breathtaking views of nature and temples, making it a popular tourist destination.

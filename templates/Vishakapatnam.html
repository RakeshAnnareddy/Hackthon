WELCOME TO KURNOOL DISTRICT

Kurnool District is known for its rich history and beautiful tourist spots. From temples to forts and natural wonders, Kurnool offers a variety of scenic destinations for travelers.

Places to visit in Kurnool

Kurnool Fort

The Kurnool Fort, built in the 16th century, offers a glimpse into the history of the region. It is famous for its impressive architecture and historical significance.

Alcott Burial Site

This historic site is where British officer Major John Alcott was buried. It is an interesting place to visit for history enthusiasts and those interested in colonial-era artifacts.

Rollapadu Wildlife Sanctuary

Rollapadu Wildlife Sanctuary is home to several endangered species, including the Great Indian Bustard. It is a haven for nature lovers and bird watchers.

Simhadri Temple

The Simhadri Temple, located near Kurnool, is dedicated to Lord Narasimha. It is an important pilgrimage site for devotees and attracts visitors with its serene atmosphere.

Banaganapalle

Known for its famous Banaganapalle mangoes, this town is a picturesque place with orchards and greenery. It's a peaceful getaway perfect for nature lovers.

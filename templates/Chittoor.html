WELCOME TO CHITTOOR DISTRICT

Chittoor, located in Andhra Pradesh, is renowned for its temples, natural beauty, and rich history. The district is a must-visit for anyone interested in cultural and spiritual experiences.

Places to visit in Chittoor

Sri Venkateswara Temple

One of the most revered pilgrimage sites in India, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara and is located in Tirupati, Chittoor. It is famous for its spiritual importance and grand architecture.

Sri Kalahasti Temple

The Sri Kalahasti Temple is a famous Shiva temple in Chittoor, known for its intricate architecture and significance as a prominent religious site. It is also known for its Vayu Linga worship.

Ashoka Tree Temple

This temple, located near Tirupati, is known for its association with the famous Ashoka tree. The temple is an important religious spot for pilgrims and nature lovers alike.
